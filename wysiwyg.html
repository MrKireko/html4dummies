Using a WYSIWYG website builder



What is WYSIWIG?

WYSIWYIG is short for 'What You See Is What You Get'. Usually it means you have a drag&drop website builder, where you can literally drag website elements to their places.


How do they work?

There are a lot of websites for WYSIWYG website creation. Most of them first have you choose a basic template for your website. Then you're brought to a place where you put your website together by dragging and dropping elements around the page.


How do you get started?

You can go to several websites (like Wix). They have fully explained tutorials on their website, go check them out!
